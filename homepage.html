Welcome to the News App! Here's how you can use the filters to find the news you want:

  • Breaking News: Click this to fetch the latest breaking news from all over the world instantly.
  • Get News: Use this after selecting filters to get customized news.

Filter Options:

  • Search: Enter keywords related to the news you're looking for.
  • Country: Choose a country to get localized news (this takes priority over category if both are selected).
  • Category: Choose a news category like sports, business, health, etc.
  • From Date & To Date: Select a date range for the news articles.
  • Sort By: Choose how results are sorted: latest, relevancy, or popularity.
  • Language: Select the language for the news. Default is English.
  • Note: If ApiKey Problem Occurs Logout and then Again Submit the Credential Carefull while filling The API KEY

Once you've selected your filters, click Get News to view your results.
